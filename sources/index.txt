A Little Book of Disasters
======================================================
The Layman's Guide to Disaster Risk Management
------------------------------------------------------

This book, written in `simple english`_, talks about disasters. It explains what they are, how they can be made to happen less, and the science used to manage them. It is written for policy and decision makers, educators, students and the general public. It is not a scientific text.

.. _simple english: http://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simple_English#Simple_English
